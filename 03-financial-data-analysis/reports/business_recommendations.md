# Financial Data Analysis – Business Recommendations

## 1. Strategic Overview

This analysis supports **portfolio construction**, **risk monitoring**, and **sector allocation** decisions. Recommendations are framed for investment advisory, risk management, and capital allocation contexts—relevant to financial services and COO Risk roles.

---

## 2. Portfolio Allocation

| Recommendation | Rationale |
|----------------|-----------|
| **Diversify across low-correlated sectors** | Reduces portfolio volatility without sacrificing return |
| **Balance risk and return** | Use Sharpe ratio to compare sectors; favor higher risk-adjusted returns |
| **Monitor volatility regimes** | Increase diversification or reduce exposure during high-volatility periods |
| **Sector rotation** | Consider rotating into sectors with improving risk-return profiles |

---

## 3. Risk Management

| Action | Purpose |
|--------|---------|
| **Set volatility targets** | Align portfolio volatility with risk appetite |
| **Use correlation for stress testing** | Correlated assets may fail together in crises |
| **Track rolling volatility** | Early warning for regime changes |
| **Document risk metrics** | Support regulatory and internal reporting |

---

## 4. Stakeholder Communication

| Stakeholder | Key Message |
|-------------|-------------|
| **Investment / Portfolio** | Risk-return scatter and correlation matrix inform allocation; diversification reduces tail risk |
| **Risk / Compliance** | Volatility and correlation support VaR, stress testing, and risk reporting |
| **Finance / Treasury** | Sector-level risk metrics support capital allocation and hedging decisions |
