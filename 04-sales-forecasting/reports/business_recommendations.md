# Sales Forecasting – Business Recommendations

## 1. Strategic Overview

Sales forecasts support **planning**, **budgeting**, and **operational coordination**. Recommendations are framed for finance, operations, and strategy teams—relevant to COO Risk roles focused on building planning and solving impediments.

---

## 2. Planning & Budgeting

| Recommendation | Rationale |
|----------------|-----------|
| **Use 12-month forecast for annual budget** | Align revenue expectations with capacity and investment |
| **Account for seasonality** | Plan inventory and staffing for peak and trough periods |
| **Set confidence intervals** | Communicate uncertainty; plan for downside scenarios |
| **Review quarterly** | Re-forecast with latest data; adjust plans as needed |

---

## 3. Operational Coordination

| Action | Purpose |
|--------|---------|
| **Share forecast with supply chain** | Ensure inventory and logistics align with demand |
| **Coordinate with finance** | Cash flow and P&L projections based on sales forecast |
| **Align with marketing** | Campaign timing to support seasonal peaks |
| **Monitor forecast accuracy** | Track MAPE/RMSE; improve model or process |

---

## 4. Next Steps

1. **Disaggregate by region/product** – Build forecasts at segment level for finer planning.
2. **Integrate external factors** – Consider promotions, economic indicators, competitor activity.
3. **Automate refresh** – Monthly or quarterly re-run with latest data.
4. **Document assumptions** – Support audit and stakeholder alignment.
